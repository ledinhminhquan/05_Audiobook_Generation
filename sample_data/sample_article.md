# The Economics of Synthetic Narration

## Background

In 2024, audiobook revenue surpassed $2.0B worldwide, up 13% year over year.
A single finished hour of human narration costs between $200 and $400, and a
typical 100,000-word book runs to roughly 11 hrs of audio.

## Why It Matters

By 9:00 AM most commuters have a 45 min window to listen. Reaching them at
1/10 of the production cost changes which titles get an audio edition at all.
